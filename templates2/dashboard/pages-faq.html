Basic Questions
1. How can Phyed help me with resume building?

Phyed offers personalized resume verification and making from scratch by industry professionals through one-on-one meetings, ensuring your resume stands out and showcases your skills effectively.

2. Can I connect with students from other colleges through Phyed?

Absolutely! Phyed connects college students from across India. You can join our student community platform, engage in discussions, collaborate on projects, and build connections with students from various colleges.

3. Are there any charges for using Phyed's services?

Phyed is committed to providing accessible services to college students. Our platform is free to use, excluding the resume-building feature. We believe in supporting students without any financial burden.
